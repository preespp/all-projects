Coin Collector Robot

BU Robotics Hackathon, Spring 2025

Project Description

This robot was built during the BU Robotics Hackathon as a one-week challenge with a team of four. Our goal was to design a fast and reliable system that could collect magnetic coins and place them into scoring bins under tight time constraints.

We developed a four-wheel robot with a magnetic collection mechanism and iterated quickly through fabrication, testing, and strategy decisions. The final robot collected 20 coins in under two minutes and helped our team finish in first place.

My Responsibilities

  • Engineered the robot concept and collection strategy with a four-person team during the one-week hackathon.
  • Developed a four-wheel robot with a magnetic mechanism for collecting coins and delivering them to bins.
  • Designed and 3D printed parts using Bambu Lab equipment.
  • Assembled hardware and performed iterative testing to improve performance.

Skills I Learned

  • Rapid prototyping for competition robotics.
  • Mechanical iteration under short deadlines.
  • Team coordination during a compressed build cycle.
  • Hands-on testing and debugging of small robot systems.

Awards

  • 1st place, BU Robotics Hackathon.
