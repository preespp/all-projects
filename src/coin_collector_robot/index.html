Coin Collector Robot

BU Robotics Hackathon, Spring 2025

Coin-Collecting Robot was built during the BU Robotics Hackathon as a one-week challenge with a team of four.

We engineered a four-wheel robot with a magnetic collection mechanism to gather magnetic coins and deliver them to scoring bins as quickly and reliably as possible.

The final system collected 20 coins in under 2 minutes and helped the team earn 1st place.

My responsibilities
  • Engineered a solution for collecting magnetic coins to bins within a 1-week challenge as a team of 4
  • Developed a 4-wheel robot with a magnetic mechanism to collect 20 coins under 2 minutes and score 1st place
  • Designed and 3D printed components using Bambu Lab, assembled parts, and performed hardware testing
