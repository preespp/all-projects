Catapult

BU SILab Catapult Competition

Outside Class Project

Project Description

This project was built for the BU SILab Catapult Competition, where teams designed and tested a catapult system to launch projectiles accurately and consistently. The competition emphasized mechanical design, iterative prototyping, and performance tuning under practical constraints.

My Responsibilities

  • Designed and built the catapult system for the BU SILab Catapult Competition.
  • Tested and iterated on the mechanism to improve launch consistency and performance.
  • Worked on prototyping, assembly, and competition preparation.

Skills I Learned

  • Mechanical prototyping and hands-on fabrication. (Wood Work)
  • Iterative testing and tuning for performance-based design.
  • Applying engineering design decisions under competition constraints.
