Analysis of the Sustainability

of the World`s Growing Population

Fall 2022

This project analyzed crop production data and population growth trends to study global sustainability challenges.

Using MATLAB and data analysis techniques, the work explored how resource production and human population changes interact over time across global regions.

The analysis used over 20,000 datasets spanning 1990 to 2022 to predict future regional trends through 2025.

My responsibilities
  • Analyzed data on crop production and population growth to address sustainability situations globally
  • Predicted future trends for each region until 2025 using raw data from more than 20,000 datasets collected during 1990-2022
  • Performed statistical analysis and machine learning in MATLAB leading to predictions across 7 continents
