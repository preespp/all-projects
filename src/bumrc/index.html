BU Mars Rover Club

Engineer & Team Lead, Sep 2023 - May 2025

BU Mars Rover Club is a student-run engineering team developing a rover for the University Rover Challenge. I contributed across robotics, electrical systems, embedded systems, and software while also taking leadership roles in the electrical team.

Leadership roles: Electrical Lead (Jan 2025 - May 2025); Electrical Deputy Lead (Sep 2024 - Jan 2025)

Technical roles: Software (Sep 2024 - Jan 2025); Electrical (Sep 2023 - May 2024); Robotics (Sep 2023 - May 2024)

   Leadership responsibilities
  • Oversaw power systems, computer networking, and embedded systems tasks
  • Taught embedded systems and electrical skills, both hands-on and theory, instructed team tasks, and reinforced lab safety
  • Managed budgets and timelines, communicated with other subteams, and reported weekly updates in E-board meetings
  • Wrote reports for Preliminary Design Review and System Acceptance Review in the electrical section
    •    Technical contributions
  • Researched Dynamixel servo motors, implemented controller encoder controls with NVIDIA Jetson, and configured the GPS module with a ROS2 node script
  • Wired circuits for the power regulation system of the Mars Rover, conducted electrical load tests, and gained proficiency in PCB design software
  • Designed robotics arm components including spacers, end-effector grippers, bearing caps, and casings, improving casing surface protection by 60% with a new design
  • Conducted assembly and functionality testing of robotics arm prototypes for participation in the University Rover Challenge
