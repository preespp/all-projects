Smart Fan

BU Hardware Hackathon, Fall 2023

Project Description

Smart Fan was a hardware hackathon project built to measure a user's heart rate and activate a fan for body cooling and stress reduction. The project combined real-time sensing, embedded programming, circuit design, and physical prototyping within a short competition timeline.

My Responsibilities

  • Rapid prototyped the product, designed the circuit, and transmitted real-time data via a biometric sensor in C++.
  • Built a smart box that detected the user's heart rate and activated a fan.
  • Soldered the circuit connecting the microcontroller, heart rate monitor, LCD display, and motor.

Skills I Learned

  • Rapid hardware prototyping for hackathons.
  • Biometric sensing and actuator control.
  • Circuit assembly and embedded debugging.

Awards

  • 3rd place in BU Hardware Hackathon, Fall 2023
