Smart Fan

BU Hardware Hackathon, Fall 2023

Smart Fan was a hardware hackathon project built to measure a user's heart rate and activate a fan for body cooling and stress reduction.

The project combined rapid prototyping, circuit design, real-time biometric sensing, and physical product assembly within a short competition timeline.

This detail page is ready for more materials later, including circuit photos, demo clips, and a fuller breakdown of the sensing logic.

My responsibilities
  • Rapid prototyped the product, designed the circuit, and transmitted real-time data via a biometric sensor in C++
  • Manufactured a smart box to detect the user's heart rate and activate a fan for body cooling and stress reduction
  • Soldered the circuit to connect the microcontroller with the heart rate monitor sensor, LCD display, and motor
