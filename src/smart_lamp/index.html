Smart Lamp

Lutron Light Engineering Competition, Spring 2024

Smart Lamp was developed for the Lutron Light Engineering Competition as a smart device that can automatically turn on and adjust brightness and color based on the distance between the user and the light source, along with the room environment.

The project combined embedded firmware development, circuit design, rapid prototyping, and sensor-driven decision making to create a more responsive lighting system.

The system included multiple electronics and lighting behaviors, with both functional and aesthetic prototyping work.

My responsibilities
  • Programmed firmware in C++ with multithreading, leading to user detection at 3 meters and 10 light patterns
  • Designed the circuit for a smart device with LCD, TMP36, NeoPixels, and ultrasonic sensors
  • Rapid prototyped the product, 3D printed and laser cut the container and external decoration using Onshape
