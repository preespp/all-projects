Truss Design Optimizer

Fall 2023

This project focused on designing and fabricating an efficient truss structure that balanced cost, weight, and load performance.

MATLAB calculations and lab testing were used to evaluate different designs and guide the final truss configuration.

The end goal was to create the most practical truss in terms of spending efficiency and maximum supported load.

My responsibilities
  • Fabricated the most effective and practical truss in terms of cost spending and maximum weight load
  • Programmed an algorithm in MATLAB to test maximum weight load and internal forces for each design
  • Performed buckling lab tests to find the critical forces for materials at each length
