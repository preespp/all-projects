Truss Design Optimizer

Fall 2023

Project Description

This project focused on designing and fabricating an efficient truss structure that balanced cost, weight, and load performance. MATLAB calculations and lab testing were used to compare designs and guide the final truss configuration.

My Responsibilities

  • Fabricated the most effective and practical truss in terms of cost and maximum supported load.
  • Programmed a MATLAB algorithm to test maximum load and internal forces for each design.
  • Performed buckling lab tests to determine critical forces for materials at different lengths.

Skills I Learned

  • Structural analysis and design optimization.
  • MATLAB modeling for engineering decisions.
  • Connecting simulation results with fabrication and lab testing.
