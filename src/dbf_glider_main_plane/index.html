DBF Glider & Main Plane

BU AIAA Design/Build/Fly Team, Fall 2024 - Spring 2025

This project was part of the BU AIAA Design/Build/Fly team for the DBF competition season.

I worked as an Electronics & Propulsion Systems Engineer, contributing to avionics-related research, circuit schematics, and power-system calculations for the glider and main plane.

My work also included report contributions, bill of materials documentation, and radio testing with motors.

My responsibilities
  • Contributed to the proposal and final report
  • Researched electronics components for avionics systems and documented the bill of materials
  • Performed calculations for the power system and designed circuit schematics for the glider and main plane using KiCad
  • Tested the radio transmitter and receiver with motors
